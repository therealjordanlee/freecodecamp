Vladimir Putin

Geopolitical Visionary

  • 1952 - Born in Leningrad, Russia.
  • 1960 - Starts school at School #193 at Baskov Lane.
  • 1975 - Graduated from Leningrad State University; Joins the KGB, initially serving in counter-intelligence.
  • 1985 - Serves in East Germany, under cover as a translator.
  • 1990 - Returns to Leningrad, serving in International Affairs. an advisor on international affairs to the Mayor of Leningrad, Anatoly Sobchak.
  • 1991 - Resigns from the KGB, with the rank of Lt. Colonel.
  • 1996 - Appointed as Deputy Chief of the Presidential Property Management Department.
  • 1997 - Appointed as Deputy chief of Presidential Staff, under President Boris Yeltsin.
  • 1998 - Appointed as Director of the Federal Security Service (FSB), the primary intelligence and security organization of the Russian Federation and the successor to the KGB.
  • 1999 - Appointed as Prime Minister of Russia.
  • 2000 - Becomes President of Russia.
  • 2004 - Wins the Presidency (second time)).
  • 2008 - Appointed Prime Minister of Russia.
  • 2012 - Becomes President of Russia (third time).
  • 2018 - Wins the Presidency (fourth time).
